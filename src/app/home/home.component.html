Welcome to Block Island Moths!


This site is a virtual collection of the moth phenology research conducted by Aaron Hunt.

Aaron Hunt has spent several years surveying the moths of Block Island, Rhode Island. His official species list currently sits at exactly 1000; this includes some species found on older surveys on the island but excludes a number of unidentified species. Aaron has sampled at locations throughout the island with the goal of determining species abundance, local distribution, and flight times.
